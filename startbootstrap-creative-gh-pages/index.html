Find More About Me

I am Kenneth Motau, from Jericho where I did my primary and secondary schooling. I have completed B.Sc. Degree at University of Limpopo (Medunsa Campus). I majored with Statistics and Computer Science. And I have completed Lean Six Sigma Black Belt with The Six Sigma Way institute in Texas (USA).

Since I finished the degree, I have been working as an individual contractor/ and freelancer focusing in multimedia, information technology, data analysing and training. I spend most of time in training and helping both lectures and students in their researches to analyse data.

I have been involved in multiple projects both nationally and internationally. The first interesting project is the development of learning material for Edcon Group. I developed data integrity manuals for different levels of retail managers. And the second one, I worked for six months in Asia, South Korea. I was assisting the research and development department of a language institute to upgrade some of the learning materials that were used to teach both juniors and adults an English language. I am at present involved with both companies and training providers. I am developing learning materials, facilitating and assessing learners. And I am also an associate consultant focusing on continuous improvements of business process in different sectors (e.g. Public Services, Mining and Manufacturing).

I am offering both training and consulting in most of the areas such as Safety, Health, Environmental, Quality, Risk, Finance, Marketing, Retails, Education, Mining, Construction, Manufacturing, Telecommunication, Science, Engineering and Technology. The main interests are as follows: To collect, process and analyse both qualitative and quantitative data; to work with scientists, engineers, technicians, educators, managers, etc. as team in education, innovations, researches and developments; to assist in problem solving and decision making; to give both mentorship and coaching leaders; to design, re-engineer, model, analyse processes in order to increase services/product yield and quality, lower costs, enhance plant safety and provide processes flexibility; to write programs that index information, analysing data, solve complex problems and display data when needed.
